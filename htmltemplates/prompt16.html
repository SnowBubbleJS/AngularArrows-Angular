Bringing in data from myFactory

In the MyController function, similarly to how we used var vm = this; before, let’s keep vm in our function, but get rid of vm.name = 'Bob';, vm.age = 30;, and vm.occupation = 'hacker';. In our MyController function, let’s bring the data from myFactory. Add vm.list = myFactory after you define vm. Now we can use list in the view to see the data from our factory.
